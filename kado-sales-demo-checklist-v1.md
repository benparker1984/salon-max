## Suggested Demo Flow

1. Open the customer joining site.
2. Create a test member.
3. Add a membership/package.
4. Open the reception check-in page.
5. Use the generated PIN to check in.
6. Open staff management.
7. Show the member list, access log, finance, marketing, and class catalogue.
8. Explain that live Stripe and production database are activated after signup.

## Suggested Offer Wording
